MAZE DODGER

Don't touch anything that moves.

Settings

You Won't: Hard speeds, but any death sends you back to Room 1.
You Can't: same — and touching a wall kills you too. Good luck with corners.
Ramp controls how much faster enemies get each level.
Your image replaces the colored square in-game. It's stored in this browser only.

How to Play

Move with WASD or the arrow keys.
Get from the start to the glowing exit without touching a moving enemy.
Touch an enemy and you're reset to the start of the room. The enemies reset too — their patterns are the same every attempt, so every death teaches you something.
Each room's enemies move differently: some pace back and forth, some orbit, some follow the corridors, some ricochet around.
Esc pauses. R restarts the room. Progress saves automatically.
Some exits are locked — grab every glowing key space in the room first, then the exit opens. Dying re-locks everything.
Watcher enemies (the ones with an eye) charge when they can see you. Break their line of sight behind a wall and they give up and head home.
Watchers with a closed eye are asleep — until you grab a key. Then they wake and hunt you for the rest of the room, walls or no walls between you.
Teleporters: step on a solid lettered ring and you're instantly moved to the matching dashed ring. One-way, and enemies can't follow. Some rooms can only be crossed this way.
Feeling confident? The You Won't and You Can't difficulties send you back to Room 1 on every death — and in You Can't, the walls kill you too.
